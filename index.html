How to Play

Learn how to connect to GrowCam Server

  1. First, download Powertunnel APP from here
  2. Then click on the "Copy Powertunnel" button.
  3. It will copy the powertunnel host link.
  4. Open the powertunnel app, click the three-line icon, and click the host settings icon.
  5. Paste the copied powertunnel host URL. Change the host update period from 12 hours to on start.
  6. Done, then open "Growtopia" and Connect!
